Unfortunately due to the way the graphs on this page are generated, they don't look great on smaller screens. You may have better luck rotating your device to a landscape orientation.

This plot shows the current vaccination rate. The dashed line represents the average rate required, from January 11, to achieve the target set out in the government's plan of vaccinating the 15 million people in priority groups 1-4 with their first dose by mid-February.

This plot shows the cumulative number of people vaccinated. The dashed line shows the trajectory needed to complete the vaccination target.

Data in both plots from before January 10 is based on interpolated weekly numbers by vaccination date. After that, numbers are daily by report date.
